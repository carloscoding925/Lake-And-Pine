· Who We Are ·

A collective rooted in the Sierra.

Lake & Pine is a wedding photography and videography collective based in the Reno-Tahoe area. We're three full-time photographers — Chava, Isma, and Juan — who came together to deliver high-quality, premium services for couples on their big day.

All three of us are seasoned wedding shooters with years of experience across Reno, Tahoe, and beyond. Chava is fully comfortable leading both photo and video at the same level. Isma brings a sharp photographer's eye to every gallery. Juan focuses on cinematic film work that holds up years later.

Together, that means your day is covered from every angle by one team that actually trusts each other.

· How We Work ·

A simple four-step approach.

From the first DM to the final gallery, you'll always know what's next. No surprises, no scrambling — just a smooth experience designed around the day that matters most.

01

Inquiry

Reach out and tell us about your day — venue, vibe, dreams, all of it. We'll respond within 24 hours.

02

Booking
